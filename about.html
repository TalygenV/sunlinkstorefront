How it works

You’re in control from start to finish

Design your perfect solar system in minutes.

Enter your address
Simply input your home address and our advanced satellite mapping technology instantly analyzes your roof's solar potential. We calculate available roof space, orientation, shading factors, and local sun exposure to determine your home's exact energy generation capacity.
Choose Your Equipment
Browse and select from premium solar panels, inverters, and mounting systems from top manufacturers. Compare specifications, warranties, and efficiency ratings to build your perfect system. See real-time pricing updates as you customize your equipment selection—no markups or hidden fees.
Design Solar Layout on Your Home
Use our interactive design tool to place panels exactly where you want them on your roof. Drag and drop panels to optimize placement, avoid obstacles like vents or chimneys, and see instant energy production estimates. Your custom layout is designed by you, for your specific needs and preferences.
Get Installed by Verified SunLink Install Partners
Once your design is complete and contracts are signed, we connect you with a pre-vetted, licensed installer in your area. Our verified partners handle all permits, utility coordination, and professional installation. Most systems are installed and activated within one day, getting you up and running quickly.
